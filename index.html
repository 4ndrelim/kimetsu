Kimetsu no Yaiba (鬼滅の刃)
Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a hit manga series by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows the journey of Tanjiro Kamado in becoming a full-fledged demon slayer after his family's demise by a demon and sister's transformation into one. The anime adaptation and movie, produced by Ufotable, a japanese anime studio, was widely received worldwide.
