Kimetsu no Yaiba (鬼滅の刃)

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a hit manga series by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows the journey of Tanjiro Kamado in becoming a full-fledged demon slayer after his family's demise and sister's transformation into a demon. The anime adaptation and the movie, produced by Ufotable, has been widely received worldwide.
